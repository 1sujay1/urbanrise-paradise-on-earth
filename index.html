Urban Rise - Paradise on Earth - Luxury 4BHk Villa

Welcome to Urbanrise Paradise on Earth — Bengaluru’s first water-superabundant villa community where luxury meets responsibility. Spread across 24 acres with just 9 villas per acre, this IGBC Platinum pre-certified enclave ensures abundant open spaces, privacy, and peace of mind.

Nestled in Gangasandra and embraced by 2190 acres of state-reserved forest, it offers an unmatched bond with nature — from walks under 1500+ trees to relaxing by a perennial stream. Choose from thoughtfully designed 4 BHK and 4 BHK + Home Office villas starting from ₹3.17 Cr, blending modern elegance with environmental intelligence. Urbanrise Paradise on Earth is more than a home — it’s a greener, healthier, future-ready lifestyle.
